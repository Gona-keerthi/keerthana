Fidaa (2017) is a 2017 Telugu romantic drama directed by Sekhar Kammula that tells the story of Bhanumathi, a bold and independent village girl, and Varun, a soft-spoken NRI software professional. Eega (2012) is a 2012 Telugu fantasy film directed by S. S. Rajamouli that tells the story of a man who is killed and reincarnated as a housefly to take revenge on his murderer. The film is known for its unique concept and groundbreaking visual effects. Nani, Samantha, and Sudeep delivered remarkable performances. Eega (2012) is celebrated for its thrilling storyline, humor, and creative action sequences. Its combination of fantasy, drama, and comedy makes it a memorable and entertaining movie.

3.Life is Beautiful – "A tale of friendship, love, and unforgettable bonds."_

Life Is Beautiful is a 2012 Telugu coming-of-age film directed by Sekhar Kammula that beautifully portrays the lives, dreams, and strong friendship of six youngsters living in a close-knit colony. The movie highlights youthful emotions, love, fun, and the importance of relationships in everyday life. Its realistic storytelling connects deeply with the audience. Natural performances by the cast and soulful music add charm to the film. Life Is Beautiful is remembered for its heartwarming depiction of friendship and life’s simple moments.
